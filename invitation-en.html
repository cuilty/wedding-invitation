How It All Started

The Unfiltered Version
2022

"Just Friends"

It started the way all great love stories do: with drinks. An economist who names his dog after econometric models and a lawyer in Mexico City walked into each other's lives. We were "just friends" — the kind who happened to talk every day, party together every chance they got, and somehow always ended up in each other's corner. Sure. Just friends.

2022 — 2024

The Long-Distance Era

Plot twist: we caught feelings. Two years, two countries, and an embarrassing amount of flights later — we made it work. Somewhere between LA and Mexico City, Emilio fell for the girl with the biggest heart he'd ever seen. The one who listens like nobody else, who'd drop everything to help a friend, and who also happens to love Captain Morgan.

2024

The Big Move

Paola said "adiós Mexico" and brought Bowie (yes, like David Bowie — he's a bulldog with excellent taste in music) to LA. Emilio and Logit (yes, like the logit model — he's an excellent econometrician) were waiting. The four of us became a family: two humans, two bulldogs, and too many movie nights on the couch.

2026

Here We Go!

After two years of living together — and confirming that we actually like each other even when there's no screen between us — we're making it official. Hawaii, here we come.
